   Overview
ThriveSync is an AI-powered platform designed for special education, therapy, and early childhood programs—especially those operating under NYSED 4410 and DOE/CPSE contracts. The system will serve as an all-in-one compliance, documentation, and management tool that connects administrators, therapists, teachers, and finance teams under one digital ecosystem.

Think of ThriveSync as a smarter, modernized version of systems like TheraNote, TheraWeb, or Billing Simplified, but fully automated with AI to reduce paperwork, ensure compliance, and help schools like Innovators Prep run efficiently.

⸻

Core Vision

To build the first AI-driven compliance and management system that helps special education providers:
• Stay compliant with NYSED, DOE, and Article 47/89 regulations
• Simplify therapy documentation and billing
• Streamline staff attendance, payroll, and scheduling
• Track student progress and IEP goals
• Generate CFR-ready financial and service reports automatically
• Manage HR, onboarding, and staff credentials
• Use AI to auto-summarize notes, detect errors, and flag compliance gaps

⸻

Core Modules

1. TheraNote (Therapy Documentation)
This will be the first feature to build.
• Therapist login: speech, OT, PT, counseling, ABA
• Session entry: daily notes, duration, goal worked on, data collection
• AI auto-summarizes notes, checks for compliance wording
• Built-in IEP goal tracking and progress graph
• Auto-calculates missed/make-up sessions
• Generates monthly session logs and audit reports

Goal: Replace paper notes and simplify DOE/CPSE documentation.

⸻

2. Admin Dashboard
• Staff management: add/edit employees, assign roles (teacher, therapist, admin)
• Class roster management: student name, DOB, IEP start/end dates, related services
• Attendance (staff & students)
• Payroll export
• DOE billing and service tracking

⸻

3. AI Compliance Assistant
• Real-time flagging of noncompliance (e.g., missing signature, wrong session length)
• Auto-audit reports for CFR preparation
• Prompt staff when deadlines or service lapses occur
• Generate letters, memos, and audit-ready summaries using AI

⸻

4. Finance Module (Phase 2)
• Allocate direct vs. non-direct costs automatically
• Expense upload and categorization
• Rent/utilities allocation by square footage
• Tuition and reimbursement tracking
• CFR and enhancement budget reports

⸻

5. Parent/Family Portal (Phase 3)
• Parents can see their child’s goals, session summaries, and attendance
• Upload consents, receive progress reports
• Chat feature for home-school communication

⸻

Technical Notes

Architecture / Stack (suggested)
• Frontend: React or Vue
• Backend: Node.js + Express or Django
• Database: PostgreSQL or Firebase (for scalability)
• AI Integration: OpenAI API (GPT-based for note summarization, compliance detection, etc.)
• Authentication: Firebase Auth or OAuth 2.0
• Hosting: AWS or Google Cloud
• Future Mobile App: React Native for cross-platform support

Phase 1 Development Plan (Focus on TheraNote MVP)

Step 1: Build Therapist Portal
• Secure login (therapist ID + password)
• Dashboard with upcoming sessions and pending notes

Step 2: Create Session Entry Form
• Fields: student name, date, service type, minutes, goal, session note

Step 3: Admin Dashboard
• Admin can assign students to therapists
• Admin can view logs by student, service, or therapist

Step 4: Reporting
• Auto-generate weekly/monthly service reports
• Filter by date, provider, or student
