This is what I have : 🧠 THERANOTE — Core Concept

TheraNote is your AI-powered clinical documentation assistant for preschool special-education and therapy providers (speech, OT, PT, counseling, ABA, SEIT, SCIS, 4410 programs).

It solves the real-world problem you live every day:

Staff spend hours writing notes, progress reports, and session logs — and quality + compliance vary.
TheraNote automates documentation while keeping it accurate, audit-ready, and aligned to DOE / NYSED / Medicaid / Article 47 requirements.

🎯 Primary Goals
   •    Reduce therapist and teacher paperwork time
   •    Improve note quality, consistency, and compliance
   •    Support real-time documentation instead of back-logging notes
   •    Protect programs from audit risk and denials
   •    Provide administrators with visibility into service delivery

🧩 Key Features (Planned)
   •    AI-assisted session notes (SOAP, narrative, DOE formats)
   •    Goal-tracking linked to IEPs & IFSPs
   •    Attendance + service logs
   •    Progress report generator
   •    Session templates by discipline
   •    Behavior incident documentation
   •    Built-in compliance prompts (frequency, duration, ratio, missed-session rules)
   •    Smart prompts for clinical language but no fabrication
   •    Offline → sync later capability for classroom & bus-limited Wi-Fi

🛡️ Compliance Orientation
   •    NYSED / DOE 4410 standards
   •    Medicaid + service billing documentation rules
   •    Audit-ready record structure
   •    Role-based permissions & HIPAA safeguards
   •    Clear “AI assist / human final signer” workflow
   •    No auto-invented data — user confirms all content before saving

You repeatedly emphasized:
AI assists, humans remain the clinical author of record.

👥 Primary Users
   •    Therapists (Speech, OT, PT, Counseling, ABA)
   •    Teachers & Classroom Teams
   •    SEITs & Paraprofessionals
   •    Program Directors & Admins
   •    Billing / Compliance Teams

🟣 THRIVESYNC — Core Concept

ThriveSync is the operations + compliance hub for special-education schools and early-childhood programs.

Where TheraNote handles clinical documentation,
ThriveSync connects the whole organization:

Staffing • Compliance • HR • Attendance • Ratios • DOE requirements • Class records • Audits • Quality assurance

You often describe it as:

“The brain of the program”

🎯 Primary Goals
   •    Centralize records + reduce spreadsheet chaos
   •    Prevent compliance violations before they happen
   •    Give directors real-time visibility
   •    Standardize operations across multiple locations
   •    Support expansion & scale
